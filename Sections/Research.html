Research to Investigate Potential Tools for Final Project

Evaluation of Real-time Workstation

Details the process of setting up and testing a real time workstation for Simulink. This workstation was used in conjunction with an Arduino Mega to control a electromagnet.

Design of a actuation & sensing systems

Details the process of setting of the camera and using it to perform background subtraction to determine the location of colored washers on a game board.        

Using a camera as a sensor and Closed-loop motor control

Details the process of using closed-loop motor control. This is then used to make the motor point to colored washers whose locations are determined by the camera.         

Work specific to Final Project

Details the process of putting everything together. The electromagnet, camera, and motor are all used in order to sort washers on a gameboard.
